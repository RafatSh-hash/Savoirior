Is the leftover food safe to eat?

Yes, absolutely! We prioritize food safety and quality. All the leftover food we offer has been prepared following strict hygiene and safety standards. We work closely with our partner restaurants to ensure that only food that meets these standards is made available for purchase.

How does the charitable donation work?

We're committed to giving back to the community. With every purchase you make, 20% of the total price is dedicated to charitable causes. This means that not only are you enjoying a delicious meal, but you're also contributing to a greater good.

Can I schedule my delivery or pickup?

At the moment, we offer a same-day delivery or pickup service. You can select your preferred option during the checkout process. We're working on expanding our delivery options in the future.

What happens if an item I ordered is sold out?

Since we offer leftover food from restaurants, availability can vary. If an item you wanted is sold out, you can choose from the other available options on the menu for that day.

What is your return or refund policy?

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you encounter any issues with your order, please contact our customer support within 24 hours of receiving your meal, and we'll be happy to assist you.

Our Mission

Minimize Waste, Maximize Taste We're on a mission to rescue gourmet leftovers from top restaurants, reducing food waste while offering affordable, quality meals to all.

Raise Awareness, Make Impact: By redistributing surplus food, we're not just feeding appetites but also contributing to food security and educating individuals about responsible food choices.

Join the journey :Join us in savoring great flavors while making a meaningful difference. "Eat to Save, Save to Eat" is our way of turning meals into moments of positive change.
